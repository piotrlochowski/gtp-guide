924/944 GTP Headlights installation Guide
924/944 > GTP Headlights > How to install

Abstract

Some time ago I started my adventure with racing... racing in my classic Porsche 944 S2. During few years I removed almost everything unnecessary interior elements, exchanged removable body metal parts with fibre glass ones and finally I wanted to buy 924 GTP Headlights. Unfortunately there was nothing that could satisfy my needs so I decided to make my own.

I had 4 main principles:

  1. Premium quality
  2. Horizontal and vertical lamp adjustment
  3. Assembly with car body using only original mounting holes
  4. Adjustable fastening for easy fitting
    5.  
  5. Tight case, resistant to rain but well ventilated

I'm pleased do present the result of my 2 years effort to reach the perfection.
